# Southwest Sweet Potato Hash

* 1 cubed sweet potato
* Diced onion
* 1 can corn, drained
* 1 can black beans, drained
* Salt
* Pepper
* Other spices?

## Steps

* Bake 30 min in cast iron skillet at 400 degrees
* Serve with salsa and guac
