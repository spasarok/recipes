## Ham Glaze

https://dinnerthendessert.com/brown-sugar-ham-glaze/

* 1 cup light brown sugar packed
* 1/2 cup orange juice
* 1/2 cup honey
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1/2 teaspoon cloves

### Steps

* Boil ingredients 3-4 minutes then add to ham and cook
