# Simple Syrups

## Cinnamon Simple Syrup

* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1 1/4 cup water

## Habanero Simple Syrup

* 2-4 habanero peppers, quartered
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1 1/4 cup water

## Rosemary Simple Syrup

* 6 rosemary sprigs
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 1/4 cup water
